# ADR 005: Sócio Solidário Plan

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The club serves a diverse community with varying levels of disposable income. Setting a high minimum price for membership maximizes per-user revenue but excludes a significant portion of the fanbase who cannot afford traditional plans, limiting the club's reach and the top-of-funnel membership base.

## Decision

We will offer and maintain a lowest-tier **"Sócio Solidário"** plan at an accessible price point, despite it generating minimal direct revenue and having low profit margins.

The existence of this plan is a strategic decision that prioritizes inclusion. It serves multiple behavioral and strategic purposes:
1. **Inclusion > Revenue:** It ensures fans from all economic backgrounds can officially belong to the club.
2. **Social Proof:** It rapidly grows the overall membership counter on the transparency portal, creating a bandwagon effect (social proof).
3. **Future Pipeline:** Fans who can only afford the solidário plan today may experience increased loyalty and upgrade to a higher tier next year when their financial situation improves.
4. **Brand Positioning:** It builds community trust and positions the club as a values-driven, community-first institution.

## Consequences

- **Positive:** Larger total membership base, amplifying social proof and community engagement.
- **Positive:** Stronger community goodwill and inclusive brand positioning.
- **Negative:** Increases the support surface area (more users) without proportionally increasing revenue.
